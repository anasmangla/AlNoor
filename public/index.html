Zabiha Standards

Hand-Slaughtered the Traditional Way

Our team follows a documented process that keeps every step halal-compliant and transparent for your family or business.

  1. Birds are health-checked, calmed with water, and positioned toward the qibla.
  2. A trained Muslim slaughterer recites the tasmiya and swiftly performs the cut with a sharpened knife.
  3. Carcasses are bled, cleaned, and chilled in dedicated equipment with tracked batch numbers.
  4. Logs record the slaughterer, inspection checks, and packaging time for complete traceability.

Need proof for your event or institution? Download the halal certificate or show the processing badge on site.
